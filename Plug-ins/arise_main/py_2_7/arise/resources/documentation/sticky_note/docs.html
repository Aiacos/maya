Sticky Note

Sticky notes function as visual aids and do not generate rig elements. Their primary purpose is to facilitate organization and convey information to your future self or teammates, regarding specific sections of the rig setup.

To create a sticky note Right-click on an empty area in the workspace and choose 'Create A Sticky-Note'.
To resize the sticky note drag the bottom-left or bottom-right corners.

Attributes: 

  • 'Align' - Buttons to align the text direction left, center, or right.
  • 'Bold' - A button to toggle the text bold.
  • 'Note' - The text that will display on the sticky note shape.
